About the College

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Kumbalathu Sankupillai Memorial Devaswom Board College (KSMDBC), established in 1964 at Sasthamcotta in Kollam district, is one of Kerala's premier government-aided arts and science colleges managed by the Travancore Devaswom Board.

Named in honour of Kumbalathu Sankupillai, a revered freedom fighter and social reformer, the college is affiliated to the University of Kerala and has been re-accredited with an 'A+' grade by NAAC, reflecting its unwavering commitment to quality education and holistic student development.
